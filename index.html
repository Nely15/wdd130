Texas

Texas is a large state in the southern part of the United States known for its diverse landscapes, strong cultural traditions, and rich history. It is the second-largest state in both size and population in the country. Texas features deserts, forests, plains, and a long coastline along the Gulf of Mexico. The state is famous for its cowboy heritage, barbecue, and major cities such as Houston, Dallas, and Austin, the state capital. Texas also plays an important role in industries like energy, technology, agriculture, and space exploration.
